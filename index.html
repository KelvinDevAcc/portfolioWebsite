ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Kelvin de Jong, a game development student at Howest in Kortrijk, Belgium. I'm passionate about creating immersive experiences, with a particular focus on VR and AR games using Unreal Engine 5 and Unity. I’m a driven and motivated learner who always aims to deliver the best possible results. During my studies, I’ve worked on a variety of projects—from fast-paced multiplayer arena games to adaptive puzzle experiences—and completed an internship at XR Lab, where I gained valuable industry insights. While I continue to develop in VR and immersive tech, I’m also gradually shifting my focus towards puzzle games, exploring their unique challenges and player engagement. Right now, I’m continuing to explore new ideas and grow as a developer through school projects and personal experimentation.
